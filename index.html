A little backstory

Hi there! I'm a 21 year old artist and aspiring creator in the music and performance industry, I've been playing instruments since I was 10 starting off with the trumpet and then moving to guitar and drums. I've been writing and producing my own music for the past 6 years and have recently just released an album with my band, Biledriver, named Let The Sun Swallow All on which I performed guitar and mixed and produced the entire record.
