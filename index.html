About Me

Hi there! I'm a 21 year old artist and aspiring creator in the music and performance industry, I've been playing instruments since I was 10 starting off with the trumpet and then moving to guitar and drums. I've been writing and producing my own music for the past 6 years and have an upcoming album with my band, Biledriver, named Let The Sun Swallow All on which I performed guitar and mixed and produced the entire record.

I also have lengthy experience in the live sound and lighting department having done many shows in the past and working for companies and venues big and small, and also closely with acts to ensure they can sound as good as possible with the equipment provided.
